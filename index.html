Investment Philosophy

I don’t try to predict—I prepare. I translate macro signals into clear rules, allocate assets by purpose, and rebalance with discipline. By combining theory and practice—focusing on risk‑adjusted outcomes—and letting compounding work over time, I pursue steady growth and long‑term value creation.

Risk First

Protect downside via position sizing, liquidity management, and rule‑based risk controls before seeking returns.

Macro + Quant

Blend macro judgment with quantitative signals across FX, commodities, and real assets; keep decisions consistent and testable.

Purposeful Allocation

Assign assets to roles—growth, income, hedge—and rebalance on a set cadence.
